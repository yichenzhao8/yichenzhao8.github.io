Brand Mascot Redesign

Duration: Jun 2017 - Feb 2019

Tool: Photoshop, Illustrator

Role: Graphic design, Animation

Design Challenge


Little ASO (阿瘦仔) was modeled after SHUI-MU International Co., Ltd.(阿瘦皮鞋)'s founder.

Though the company has made shoes and sold shoes for over 65 years in Taiwan, over the years, little ASO were not being used in advertising. With the marketing world dominated by digital media, we considered it a great time to breathe new life to the company and build an emotional connection with the audience to strengthen brand identity.

However, there were problems that made the previous design not suitable for social media engagement and needed to be redesign:
1) In the previous design, it has enormous head in relation to the size of the body. The head size was too big that audiences usually focus on the head which was not important.
2) The original design had a unhealthy looking dull skin and dark color clothes, which violate the original intent behind the company. The purpose of the SHUI-MU International Co., Ltd. Fund is to provide right shoes for customers and keep customer's feet healthy.

Activity Brochure


Mini manager is a one day camp for kids 4-12 years old. It aims to impart to kid participants the importance of foot care knowledge and spirit of service through various fun learning activities as well as building strong family relationship.

In this project, the brochure was a challenge guide map designed for kids in the activity.

Marketing Graphics

Little ASO for social media engagement was a great marketing tool that allow the company to communicate and engage with customers. Therefore, the mascot was highly used in foot care knowledge sharing, festival and advertising in social media to help connect with audience and entice in new customers.
