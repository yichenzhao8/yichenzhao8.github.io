FunNow Mobile Web Redesign

Duration: March-May 2017

Tool: Photoshop, Sketch

Role: UIUX Design Intern

Design Challenge


FunNow is an app that provide instant booking service for leisure and entertainment activities. For this project, the redesign aims to solve the following core problem:

Users only view a small amount of content at a time before having to scroll - Users visit funnow website are mainly for instant booking and want to see as more content as possible. Single column layout doesn't allow more content to be seen at a time.

Users don't have better experience with infinite scrolling in product page - Although infinite scrolling allows users to scroll through a chunk of content that automaticially refresh the page, users don't know the exact number of search results and have to spend time to find the product they just scrolled.

Content in booking page is unorganized - It is difficult to navigate the layout at a glance and quickly scan to find the information they're looking for. Users would have had trouble distinguishing where one section stops and another begins.

Design Process


Research
Got feedback from end-users, trawled the forums online and analyzed how related competitors tackled the problems.

Brainstorming + Draft
Arranged the content based on principals of visual hierarchy to guide the reader's eyes to the most important information, came up with other solutions from the problems, created ideation sketching and consulted with developers to gauge the level of difficulty in the implementation.

Design, Test, Iterate, Test, Design again
Tested the solution with users, solved the issues and designed more continued until the final design was set.
